David Bowie

Legendary English Singer

Picture of David Bowie in a leather jacket.

Here's a timeline of David Bowie's life:

  • 1947 - David Robert Jones (stage name - David Bowie) was born on January 8th in Brixton, London.
  • 1953 - Bowie's family moves to Bromley, Kent.
  • 1959 - Bowie receives his first instrument - a plastic saxophone, for Christmas.
  • 1966 - The stage name, David Bowie, is born.
  • 1967 - Bowie releases his self-titled debut album.
  • 1969 - Bowie releases his second album, Space Oddity.
  • 1970 - Bowie releases his third album, The Man Who Sold The World.
  • 1971 - Bowie releases his fourth album, Hunky Dory.
  • 1972 - Bowie releases his fifth album, The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars
  • 1973 - Bowie releases his sixth and seventh albums, Aladdin Sane and Pin Ups.
  • 1974 - Bowie releases his eighth album, Diamond Dogs.
  • 1975 - Bowie releases his ninth album, Young Americans.
  • 1976 - Bowie releases his tenth album, Station to Station.
  • 1977 - Bowie releases his eleventh album, Low and his twelvth album, Heroes.
  • 1979 - Bowie releases thirteenth album, Lodger.
  • 1980 - Bowie releases his fourteenth album, Scary Monsters.
  • 1983 - Bowie releases his fifteenth album, Let's Dance.
  • 1984 - Bowie releases his sixteenth album, Tonight.
  • 1986 - Bowie stars as the Goblin King in Jim Henson's film, Labyrinth.
  • 2016 - Bowie dies of cancer at age 69. He also released his final album that same year.
